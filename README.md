# Olist E-Commerce Analysis

## Overview
Analysis of Brazilian e-commerce data from Olist, covering 100,000+ orders from 2016 to 2018. This project explores customer behaviour, seller performance, delivery trends, and revenue patterns to support business decision-making.

## Key Findings
- **96,478 total orders** analysed across the dataset
- **São Paulo** is the top performing state by orders and revenue
- **Bed, Bath & Table** is the best-selling product category
- **Average delivery time** of 12 days with significant regional variation
- **Credit card** is the dominant payment method (74% of orders)
- **Customer satisfaction** averages 4.09 out of 5

## Tools Used
- **Python** — Data cleaning, analysis and visualisation (pandas, matplotlib, seaborn)
- **Power BI** — Interactive sales and performance dashboard
- **SQL (PostgreSQL)** — Database querying and business analysis
